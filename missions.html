Space Mission

VOYAGER 1

DETAILS:

Agency: NASA

Launch Date: September 5, 1977

Objective: Explore the outer Solar System and interstellar space.

DESCRIPTION:

Voyager 1 is the farthest human-made object from Earth. It explored the outer planets and now travels through interstellar space, carrying a message from humanity.

APOLLO 11

DETAILS:

Agency: NASA

Launch Date: July 16, 1969

Achieve the first crewed landing on the Moon.

DESCRIPTION:

Apollo 11 was the first mission to land humans on the Moon. Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin walked its surface, marking a giant leap for mankind.

Hubble Space Telescope (HST)

DETAILS:

Agency: NASA / ESA

Launch Date: April 24, 1990

Objective: Conduct high-resolution astronomy from Earth's orbit.

DESCRIPTION:

The Hubble Space Telescope orbits Earth, capturing stunning images of distant galaxies and nebulae. Its discoveries have deepened our understanding of the universe.

Curiosity (MSL)

DETAILS:

Agency: NASA

Launch Date: November 26, 2011

Objective: Search for evidence of past life conditions on Mars.

DESCRIPTION:

Curiosity explores the surface of Mars to study its climate, geology, and potential for past life. Its discoveries continue to shape our understanding of the Red Planet.

James Webb Space Telescope (JWST)

DETAILS:

Agency: NASA / ESA / CSA

Launch Date: December 25, 2021

Objective: Observe the first galaxies and exoplanets..

DESCRIPTION:

The James Webb Space Telescope peers deep into space to observe the first galaxies and stars. Its advanced instruments reveal the origins and evolution of the universe.

Perseverance (Mars 2020)

DETAILS:

Agency: NASA

Launch Date: July 30, 2020

Objective: Collect rock samples on Mars for return to Earth.

DESCRIPTION:

Perseverance explores Mars to search for signs of past life and collect samples. Its mission helps pave the way for future human exploration of the Red Planet.

Lucy

DETAILS:

Agency: NASA

Launch Date: October 16, 2021

Objective: Explore the Trojan asteroids near Jupiter.

DESCRIPTION:

Lucy is studying the Trojan asteroids that orbit near Jupiter. Its mission aims to understand the building blocks of the planets and the early history of our solar system.

Artemis I

DETAILS:

Agency: NASA

Launch Date: November 16, 2022

Objective: Uncrewed test flight of the new Orion spacecraft to the Moon.

DESCRIPTION:

Artemis I is an uncrewed mission testing the Space Launch System and Orion spacecraft. It paves the way for future human exploration of the Moon and beyond.
